Dan's Crossword WebApp

About

This webapp takes an Across Lite file (.puz or .txt) and generates a playable crossword puzzle in the browser. You can click on one of the links below to load a rejected puzzle, or you can upload a puzzle you have in an Across Lite format. (Rebus puzzles likely won't work.)

The webapp is intended to be desktop/laptop and iPhone friendly. (It probably doesn't work on other phones.)

Reference Highlighting

What a Cool Feature!

My webapp has an innovative feature I call Reference Highlighting. When you are working on a clue like "See 28-Down" then 28-Down will get highlighted as well and its clue will be displayed. It doesn't take any additional work from you (or the constructor) as long as the reference is made in the conventional way. It even works when a single clue references multiple clues.
